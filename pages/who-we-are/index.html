Who We Are

A.K.O.R. is an education- and research-informed initiative dedicated to strengthening Haitian families in Haiti and the diaspora through positive, culturally relevant parenting education grounded in democratic values.

Our Mission

Partner with community leaders in Haiti and the diaspora to build lasting capacity for culturally relevant, research-based AKOR parenting education that strengthens families and supports a more just society.

Our Vision

Haitian families everywhere thrive in nurturing, just, and resilient relationships—raising empowered children and strengthening their communities.

Our Values

Our work is grounded in the AKOR principles of Encouragement, Consequences, Honor, and Respect. We are evidence-informed, culturally relevant, and partnership-driven—building community capacity to strengthen Haitian families in Haiti and across the diaspora and to support relationships that reflect dignity, resilience, and justice.

What We Believe

  • Positive parenting strengthens families and protects children from the ills of violence, delinquency, substance abuse, and mental illness.
  • Parents can learn skills that foster respect, cooperation, and healthy relationships at home and in their communities.
  • Parents play a critical role in the transition from oppressive to democratic societies by teaching and modeling positive interactions with their children.
  • Parenting education can help migrant families adjust to democratic host societies.
