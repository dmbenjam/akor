What We Do

This is a culturally sensitive program that teaches parents how to develop positive relationships with their children to help them become responsible citizens who can make meaningful contributions to their society.

A.K.O.R…

  • …is based on years of Action research, universal theory, and the experience of the author
  • …was developed by Haitian professionals, with, and for Haitian parents
  • …has been successful with multicultural groups of parents
  • …offers a wealth of audio-visual materials, and delivery tools

These materials have undergone a systematic process of peer review involving highly qualified professionals at every step of their production.
