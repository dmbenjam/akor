AKOR

Ankourajman, Konsekans, Onè, Respè

A culturally relevant parenting education program based on action research with Haitian parents.

The Challenge

Haitian parents raised in authoritarian traditions face a major cultural transition — both in Haiti and abroad — as they adapt to societies where open dialogue between parents and children is expected.

Without culturally informed guidance, families risk court involvement, erosion of trust, and disconnection from schools that do not share their language or cultural context. AKOR fills that gap with research-based, culturally and linguistically relevant resources that meet Haitian families where they are.

Our IDEA Framework

The four pillars of our approach to strengthening families and communities.

We support the widespread implementation of the AKOR curriculum through parenting workshops delivered in schools, community centers, and churches in Haiti and throughout the diaspora.

We continually develop and refine materials that enrich the workshop experience, while exploring ways to expand access to our content through digital platforms, including messaging tools and social media.

We train and support community leaders to deliver parenting workshops and family empowerment activities, building local capacity and ensuring cultural relevance and trust.

We evaluate the impact of our interventions using qualitative feedback from participants and quantitative measures, allowing us to learn, adapt, and strengthen the program over time.

Our Founder

GB

Dr. Guilhène Benjamin, Founder

Dr. Guilhène Benjamin began her career at a young age and has drawn from enriching experiences in both her home country, Haiti, and the United States. Now retired, she devotes her time to the pursuit of an ideal she formed early in her career: to connect Haitian parents with culturally and linguistically relevant resources that can help them heal from the oppressive traditions of their upbringing, adjust to the democratic context of host societies where they migrate, and bring positive change in their country of origin. Dr. Benjamin developed the AKOR parenting education program through years of action research with parents in Miami-Dade schools. She is the proud founder of AKOR Parenting, which she leads in collaboration with educators, psychologists, and university partners across Miami and Haiti.
